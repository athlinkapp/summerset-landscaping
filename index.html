Our Story

Rooted in craft.
Driven by pride
in the work.

Summerset Landscaping was built on the belief that your property deserves the same care and attention to detail that goes into a well-crafted home. Since day one, we've approached every job — from a simple lawn trim to a full-scale outdoor living space — with precision and ownership.

We're a local, family-operated crew. No subcontractors, no shortcuts. When you hire Summerset, you get the same experienced hands on every visit — people who take personal pride in the work they leave behind.

How It Works

From first call
to finished site

We keep things clear and simple. No guessing, no surprise bills — just a clean process from consultation to completion.

01
Site Walkthrough
We meet on your property, assess the space, and listen to your goals. No pressure, no pitch — just an honest look at what's possible.
02
Custom Proposal
A detailed, line-item quote arrives within 48 hours. Scope, materials, timeline, and final price — fully transparent before a single shovel hits the ground.
03
Skilled Execution
Our crew shows up on schedule and works with the same precision every single visit. We treat your property like it's our own — because our name is on it.
04

We do a final walk with you before calling any job complete. If something isn't right, we fix it. Simple as that.
